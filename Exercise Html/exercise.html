Step by Step instructions for Step

Instructions

Step aerobics is a terrific and low-impact way to burn fat and get fit, very similar to walking. Here are some ways to make those first few classes a little less intimidating.

Steps Involved

Step by Step Guide to Step

  1. Stand near something you want to step onto.

  2. Stand up straight.

  3. No arching back.

  4. Lean from below the ankles when you step onto platform. Don't lean forward from anywhere else.

  5. Ensure whole foot touches the ground no tippy toes.

  6. No stomping.

  7. Keep jelly knees no locking in place.

  8. Now march.Left right left right. Swap lead leg or lose count.

  9. Make sure you rock your feet when you come off the platform.

Variations

Here are a few variations that you might like to try.

Alternating V-Step

Begin with one foot leading wide, then tap with the opposite foot and step wide with that foot.

Example: Step wide with the R foot leading followed by wide with the left foot. Step down toward the middle with the R foot, followed by the L foot tapping and then stepping wide.

Knee Lift

Step with one foot and lift the opposite knee. (Can be done with the right or left leg leading)

Example: Step with the R foot and lift the L knee.

Alternating Curls

Example: Step with the R foot and curl the L leg (bring the heel to your rear). Place the L foot on the floor followed by the R foot. Step with the L foot and curl the R leg.
